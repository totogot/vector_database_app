Hebbia has experienced significant revenue growth, with its annualized recurring revenue (ARR) surging from $0.47 million in 2022 to $10 million in 2023, marking an impressive 2046% growth. The company, valued at $700 million during its Series B round in July 2024, has raised $161.1 million in total, with contributions from investors like Andreessen Horowitz and Google Ventures. Hebbia provides AI-powered document analysis software primarily to financial institutions, law firms, and government agencies. It has achieved notable market traction, especially in private equity due diligence, reporting 90% penetration among top private equity firms. While part of its revenue comes from pilot contracts, it indicates strong product-market fit within its target sectors.
